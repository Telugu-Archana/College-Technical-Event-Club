Computer Society Of India

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is a non-profit organization representing computer professionals in India. Established in 1965, it has 72 chapters across the country, 511 student branches, and 100,000 members. The CSI is dedicated to the advancement of Computer Engineering and Technology Systems, Science and Engineering, Information Processing, and related Arts and Sciences. The organization encourages professionals to maintain integrity and competence, fostering a sense of partnership among members. CSI also conducts periodic conferences and seminars. Since its inception, CSI has been in close liaison with the International Federation for Information Processing (IFIP) and has organized many IFIP sponsored events. The organization represents India in technical committees and working groups of IFIP. Prof. Vipin Tyagi, from Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, serves as the Hon. Secretary of the Computer Society of India.
